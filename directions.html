From Richmond and North

  1. Start out heading SOUTH on US-27.
  2. In Liberty bear onto 101 SOUTH.
  3. Follow about 17 miles to Brookville.
  4. Turn RIGHT onto 6th Street.
  5. Follow 6-7 miles, church is on the left.

From Indianapolis and West:

  1. Start out going EAST on I-74.
  2. Take the Batesville exit - EXIT 149.
  3. Turn LEFT onto IN-229/MAIN ST. Continue to follow IN-229.
  4. At the Hansen Center, 229 veers left; turn RIGHT there onto Six Pine Branch Road. Follow closely
  5. Continue straight after the four-way stop (becomes Morris Road), Follow to the end (T-section).
  6. Turn RIGHT on St. Mary's Road.
  7. After passing St. Mary's Church, Ss. Philomena and Cecilia will be about 4 miles on the right.

From Sunman and South

  1. 1: Start out going NORTH on IN-101.
  2. 2: IN-101 becomes ST MARY RD (follow road CLOSELY, go about 10 miles after I-74 overpass; bear left at the fork about a half mile after the overpass.).
  3. 3: Church will be on the right.

From Cincinnati and East:

  1. Start out going WEST on I-74.
  2. Take the Us-52 exit - EXIT 169 - toward BROOKVILLE.
  3. Turn LEFT onto US 52 (follow road about 13 miles after into Brookville).
  4. Turn LEFT onto 6TH STREET.
  5. Follow 6-7 miles, church is on the left.
