Ss. Philomena and Cecilia Catholic Church

Ss. Philomena and Cecilia Catholic Church is served by the Priestly Fraternity of St. Peter with the approval and blessing of His Excellency Most Rev. Joseph W. Tobin, C.Ss.R. All Masses and Sacraments are performed in the Latin extraordinary form of the Roman Rite, in accordance with the Missal of Blessed John XXIII published in 1962, as expressed in the Motu Proprio Summorum Pontificum of His Holiness Pope Benedict XVI.
